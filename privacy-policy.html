6. AI features and third-party providers

Some Products use AI language models to generate outputs (summaries, reports, analysis). We are a software publisher only — we do not operate, train, or control any AI model.

  • Free plan: AI processing is provided by Cloudflare Workers AI (Meta Llama model hosted by Cloudflare, Inc.). Content you submit for synthesis is processed by Cloudflare under their privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy. We do not store this content on our servers.
  • PRO plan — Bring Your Own Key (BYOK): If you provide your own API key, your content is transmitted directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic). Your API key and content never pass through our servers. ORDA AI SOLUTIONS acts solely as a conduit — we provide the interface only. Your use of that provider is governed by their own terms and privacy policy. We bear no responsibility for the outputs, accuracy, or availability of any third-party AI provider.
AI-generated content disclaimer. AI outputs may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for your purpose. Always verify AI-generated content before relying on it for any important decision. We accept no liability for decisions made on the basis of AI-generated outputs.

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS 1.2 minimum;
  • Data in Cloudflare KV is encrypted at rest;
  • API secrets are stored in Cloudflare secret management — never in source code;

  • Rate limiting is applied at email level to prevent abuse and credential stuffing.

12. Your rights (UK GDPR)

Under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to:

  • Access (Art. 15): request a copy of personal data we hold about you;
  • Rectification (Art. 16): request correction of inaccurate data;
  • Erasure (Art. 17): request deletion, subject to legal retention obligations;
  • Restriction (Art. 18): request that we limit processing;
