Quick answers

Things people ask.

How fast can you start?
Usually within a few days. I'm working evenings and weekends, so projects spread over 1–3 weeks depending on size. I'll give you a real timeline before you pay anything.
What if I don't know what I want exactly?
Describe the problem in plain words. "We waste hours doing X." That's enough. I'll suggest a few approaches and what each would cost.
Why are your prices lower than agencies?
No overhead. No project managers. No sales team. Just me, a laptop, and modern AI tools that let me move fast. You get what an agency would deliver, minus the markup.
Will I own what you build?
Yes. Code, docs, deployed system — all yours. No vendor lock-in. If you want another developer to take over in 6 months, they can.
What about ongoing maintenance?
Most small projects don't need it. For bigger builds, optional £50–£150/month covers small fixes and tweaks. Pause anytime, no contracts.
